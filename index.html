About Our Tropical Island

Utila, a beautiful tropical island of Honduras nestled in the Caribbean sea. Utila is about eighteen miles from the mainland port of La Ceiba, Honduras. The island is eleven kilometers long and four kilometers wide at most; the island is surrounded by a vast coral reef with a large, dynamic marine life. Utila is known to be the smallest, least expensive and most beautiful island of the Bay Island group. Historical and archeological, evidence of the Bay Islands indicates that they were inhabited well before the Europeans arrived, as long ago as 600AD by a pre-Columbian culture known as the Paya.
